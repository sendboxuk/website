How it's working

Step by step a detailed flow of the route of an email

Your application(s)

Your application prepares the data variables and calls the API. You may use a template or a product/service. You need to deliver at least a transaction ID for your email and a targeted email.

Generating and sending the email

Either choose a product or a template, the system will pick up the correct template and populate your email. SendBox saves the email in the database to resend in a further request.

Auditing and resending the emails

You may report the API calls and emails in the database. If you need to resend an email, you may resend the email. If you had changed the template or email placeholders, it will fail to send the email.
